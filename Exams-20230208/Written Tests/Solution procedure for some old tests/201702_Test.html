By making use of the semi-monocoque scheme, determine: the shear flows and the shear stresses acting on the panels, and the axial force carried by the stringers for the section at \(\xi=900\, \text{mm}\).

Data
\(d = 400\, \text{mm}\)
\(l = 2000\, \text{mm}\)
\(p_0 = 2\text{e-}2\, \text{N/mm}^2\)
\(a = 200\, \text{mm}\)
\(b = 200\, \text{mm}\)
\(c = 300\, \text{mm}\)
\(t = 4\, \text{mm}\)
\(h = 20\, \text{mm}\)
All the panels have thickness \(0.8\, \text{mm}\).

Solution

1. Stringer Area and Lumped Areas

Stringer area:

\[A_{st} = th + t(h-t) = 4 \times 20 + 4 \times (20-4) = 144\, \text{mm}^2\]

Lumped areas (stringers numbered 1-6, with 1,2,3 on top and 6,5,4 on bottom):

\[A_1 = A_6 = A_{st} + \frac{1}{2}(b+a)t = 144 + \frac{1}{2}(200+200) \times 0.8 = 304\, \text{mm}^2\] \[A_3 = A_4 = A_{st} + \frac{1}{2}(a+c)t = 144 + \frac{1}{2}(200+300) \times 0.8 = 344\, \text{mm}^2\] \[A_2 = A_5 = 2A_{st} + \frac{1}{2}(a+b+c)t = 2 \times 144 + \frac{1}{2}(200+200+300) \times 0.8 = 568\, \text{mm}^2\]

2. Section Properties

The neutral axis \(x\) passes through the center due to symmetry of the section.

First moments of area:

\[S_{x_1} = -S_{x_6} = A_1 \cdot \frac{a}{2} = 304 \times 100 = 30400\, \text{mm}^3\] \[S_{x_2} = -S_{x_5} = A_2 \cdot \frac{a}{2} = 568 \times 100 = 56800\, \text{mm}^3\] \[S_{x_3} = -S_{x_4} = A_3 \cdot \frac{a}{2} = 344 \times 100 = 34400\, \text{mm}^3\]

Second moment of area:

\[J_{xx} = 2(A_1 + A_2 + A_3)\left(\frac{a}{2}\right)^2 = 2(304 + 568 + 344) \times 100^2 = 24320000\, \text{mm}^4\]

3. External Loads and Internal Actions

Equivalent distributed load:

\[q = p_0 \cdot \frac{b+c}{2} = 0.02 \times \frac{200+300}{2} = 5\, \text{N/mm}\]

Assuming loads are introduced by ribs at spacing \(d\), the internal actions at \(\xi = 900\, \text{mm}\) are:

Shear force:

\[T = -qd\left(2 + \frac{1}{2}\right) = -\frac{5}{2}qd = -\frac{5}{2} \times 5 \times 400 = -5000\, \text{N}\]

Bending moment:

\[M = -qd \times 1050 = -5 \times 400 \times 1050 = -2100000\, \text{Nmm}\]

6. Axial Forces in the Stringers

The axial forces are calculated using:

\[F_i = A_i \cdot \frac{M}{J_{xx}} \cdot y_i\]

Top stringers (in compression, \(F_i < 0\)):

\[F_1 = A_1 \cdot \frac{M}{J_{xx}} \cdot \frac{a}{2} = 304 \times \frac{-2100000}{24320000} \times 100 = -2625\, \text{N}\] \[F_2 = A_2 \cdot \frac{M}{J_{xx}} \cdot \frac{a}{2} = 568 \times \frac{-2100000}{24320000} \times 100 = -4904\, \text{N}\] \[F_3 = A_3 \cdot \frac{M}{J_{xx}} \cdot \frac{a}{2} = 344 \times \frac{-2100000}{24320000} \times 100 = -2970\, \text{N}\]

Bottom stringers (in tension, by symmetry):

\[F_6 = -F_1 = +2625\, \text{N}\] \[F_5 = -F_2 = +4904\, \text{N}\] \[F_4 = -F_3 = +2970\, \text{N}\]
