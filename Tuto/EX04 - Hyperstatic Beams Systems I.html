LAB 4: Hyperstatic Beams Systems I

Principle of Complementary Virtual Work (PCVW) for Statically Indeterminate Beams

Theoretical Foundation

Principle of Complementary Virtual Work (PCVW):

\[\delta W_i = \delta W_e\]

For beams, the external virtual work is:

\[\delta W_e = (\delta F) \cdot (u) + (\delta M) \cdot (\varphi)\]

where virtual forces/moments act on real displacements/rotations

The internal virtual work for a system of \(i\) beams (Euler beam assumption):

\[\delta W_i = \int_V \delta \underline{\sigma} : \underline{\varepsilon} \, dV = \sum_i \int_\ell \left( T'_{xi} \frac{T_{xi}}{GA^*} + T'_{yi} \frac{T_{yi}}{GA^*} + T'_{zi} \frac{T_{zi}}{EA} + M'_{xi} \frac{M_{xi}}{EJ_{xx}} + M'_{yi} \frac{M_{yi}}{EJ_{yy}} + M'_{zi} \frac{M_{zi}}{GJ_p} \right) dz_i\]

Note: It is not mandatory to have the same constraints in the REAL and VIRTUAL systems.

Problem 1: Point Load on Hyperstatic Beam

A beam with a fixed support at point B and a roller support at point A is subjected to a point load F at the free end. Determine the reaction force at point A using the Principle of Complementary Virtual Work.

Given Data:

  • \(\ell = 4000 \text{ mm}\)
  • \(F = 6000 \text{ N}\)
  • \(E = 200{,}000 \text{ MPa}\)
  • \(I_{xx} = 500{,}000 \text{ mm}^4\)

Structural Analysis:

\[3 \text{ rigid DoF} < 4 \text{ Constraints} \rightarrow \text{Hyperstatic}\]

We cannot compute the reaction forces by simply imposing equilibrium.

Solution

Step 1: Reaction Forces Setup

REAL System

At point B (fixed support):

  • \(R_{zB}\), \(R_{yB}\), \(RM_{xB}\)

At point A (roller support):

  • \(R_{yA}\) (unknown to find)

Applied load: \(F\) at free end

VIRTUAL System

Apply unit load at A:

\[\begin{cases} R'_{zB} = 0 \\ R'_{yB} = -1 \\ RM'_{xB} = -1 \cdot 2\ell \end{cases}\]

Equilibrium equations (REAL system):

\[\begin{cases} R_{zB} = 0 \\ R_{yB} + R_{yA} + F = 0 \\ RM_{xB} + R_{yA} \cdot 2\ell + F \cdot 3\ell = 0 \end{cases}\]

2 equations, 3 unknowns → Undetermined (need PCVW)

Step 2: Internal Actions

Section 1 (from free end, \(0 \leq z_1 \leq \ell\)):

REAL
\[\begin{cases} T_{z1} = 0 \\ T_{y1} = -F \\ M_{x1} = -F \cdot z_1 \end{cases}\]
VIRTUAL (Unloaded)
\[\begin{cases} T'_{z1} = 0 \\ T'_{y1} = 0 \\ M'_{x1} = 0 \end{cases}\]

Section 2 (from A toward B, \(0 \leq z_2 \leq 2\ell\)):

REAL
\[\begin{cases} T_{y2} = -R_{yA} - F \\ M_{x2} = -R_{yA} \cdot z_2 - F \cdot (\ell + z_2) \end{cases}\]
VIRTUAL
\[\begin{cases} T'_{y2} = -1 \\ M'_{x2} = -1 \cdot z_2 \end{cases}\]

Step 3: Apply PCVW

External virtual work (displacement at A in direction of virtual unit load):

\[\delta W_e = \delta F \cdot u = 1 \cdot 0 = 0\]

Internal virtual work:

\[\delta W_i = \int_0^\ell M'_{x1} \cdot \frac{M_{x1}}{EJ_{xx}} \, dz_1 + \int_0^{2\ell} M'_{x2} \cdot \frac{M_{x2}}{EJ_{xx}} \, dz_2\]

Since \(M'_{x1} = 0\), the first integral vanishes:

\[\delta W_i = \frac{1}{EJ_{xx}} \int_0^{2\ell} (-z_2) \cdot (-R_{yA} \cdot z_2 - F\ell - Fz_2) \, dz_2 = 0\]

Expanding:

\[\int_0^{2\ell} (R_{yA} \cdot z_2^2 + F\ell z_2 + Fz_2^2) \, dz_2 = 0\]

Integrating:

\[\left[ \frac{1}{3} R_{yA} \cdot z_2^3 + \frac{1}{2} F\ell z_2^2 + \frac{1}{3} F z_2^3 \right]_0^{2\ell} = 0\]

Evaluating at limits:

\[\frac{8}{3} R_{yA} \cdot \ell^3 + 2F\ell^3 + \frac{8}{3} F\ell^3 = 0\]

Simplifying (divide by \(\ell^3\)):

\[\frac{8}{3} R_{yA} + 2F + \frac{8}{3} F = 0\]

Solving for \(R_{yA}\):

\[R_{yA} = -\frac{14}{8} F = -\frac{7}{4} F\]
Final Answer: \[\boxed{R_{yA} = -\frac{14}{8} F = -7000 \text{ N}}\]

(Negative sign indicates the reaction acts downward)

Problem 2: Distributed Load on Hyperstatic Beam

A beam with a fixed support at point B and a roller support at point A is subjected to a uniformly distributed load p over the right segment. Determine the reaction force at point A.

Given Data:

  • \(\ell = 4000 \text{ mm}\)
  • \(p = 1 \text{ N/mm}\)
  • \(E = 200{,}000 \text{ MPa}\)
  • \(I_{xx} = 500{,}000 \text{ mm}^4\)

Structural Analysis:

\[3 \text{ DoF} < 4 \text{ Constraints} \rightarrow \text{Hyperstatic}\]

Solution

Step 1: Reaction Forces Setup

REAL System

Equilibrium equations:

\[\begin{cases} R_{yB} + R_{yA} + 3p\ell = 0 \\ RM_{xB} = -3p\ell \cdot \frac{3\ell}{2} - R_{yA} \cdot 2\ell \end{cases}\]
VIRTUAL System

Unit load at A:

\[\begin{cases} R'_{yB} = -1 \\ RM'_{xB} = -1 \cdot 2\ell \end{cases}\]

Step 2: Internal Actions

Section 1: Virtual system is unloaded → No virtual work contribution

Section 2 (from A, \(0 \leq z_2 \leq 2\ell\)):

REAL
\[\begin{cases} T_{y2} = -R_{yA} - p(\ell + z_2) \\ M_{x2} = -R_{yA} \cdot z_2 - \frac{1}{2} p(\ell + z_2)^2 \end{cases}\]
VIRTUAL
\[\begin{cases} T'_{y2} = -1 \\ M'_{x2} = -1 \cdot z_2 \end{cases}\]

Step 3: Apply PCVW

External virtual work:

\[\delta W_e = 0\]

Internal virtual work:

\[\delta W_i = \frac{1}{EJ_{xx}} \int_0^{2\ell} M'_{x2} \cdot M_{x2} \, dz_2 = 0\]

Substituting:

\[\frac{1}{EJ_{xx}} \int_0^{2\ell} (-z_2) \cdot \left(-R_{yA} \cdot z_2 - \frac{1}{2} p(\ell + z_2)^2\right) dz_2 = 0\]

Expanding \((\ell + z_2)^2 = \ell^2 + 2\ell z_2 + z_2^2\):

\[\int_0^{2\ell} \left(R_{yA} \cdot z_2^2 + \frac{1}{2} p\ell^2 z_2 + p\ell z_2^2 + \frac{1}{2} p z_2^3\right) dz_2 = 0\]

Integrating:

\[\left[ \frac{1}{3} R_{yA} z_2^3 + \frac{1}{4} p\ell^2 z_2^2 + \frac{1}{3} p\ell z_2^3 + \frac{1}{8} p z_2^4 \right]_0^{2\ell} = 0\]

Evaluating:

\[\frac{8}{3} R_{yA} \ell^3 + p\ell^4 + \frac{8}{3} p\ell^4 + 2p\ell^4 = 0\]

Solving for \(R_{yA}\):

\[R_{yA} = -\frac{17}{8} p\ell\]
Final Answer: \[\boxed{R_{yA} = -\frac{17}{8} p\ell = -2125 \text{ N}}\]

Problem 3: Beam with Slider Constraint

A beam with a fixed support at point B and a slider constraint at point A is subjected to a point load F at the midpoint. The slider permits y-translation and x-rotation but prevents x-translation. Determine all reaction forces at point A.

Given Data:

  • \(\ell = 4000 \text{ mm}\)
  • \(F = 6000 \text{ N}\)
  • \(E = 200 \text{ GPa}\)
  • \(I_{xx} = 500{,}000 \text{ mm}^4\)

Structural Analysis:

\[3 \text{ DoF} < 5 \text{ Constraints} \rightarrow \text{Hyperstatic}\]

We will need 2 virtual systems to solve for the two unknowns at A.

Solution

Step 1: Reaction Forces Setup

REAL System:

\[\begin{cases} R_{zB} = 0 \\ R_{yB} = -F - R_{yA} \\ RM_{xB} = -F\ell - R_{yA} \cdot 2\ell - RM_{xA} \end{cases}\]
VIRTUAL System 1

Unit force at A:

\[\begin{cases} R'_{yB} = -1 \\ RM'_{xB} = -2\ell \end{cases}\]
VIRTUAL System 2

Unit moment at A:

\[RM''_{xB} = -1\]

Step 2: Internal Actions - Section 1 (\(0 \leq z_1 \leq \ell\))

REAL:

\[\begin{cases} T_{y1} = -R_{yA} \\ M_{x1} = -R_{yA} \cdot z_1 - RM_{xA} \end{cases}\]
VIRTUAL 1
\[\begin{cases} T'_{y1} = -1 \\ M'_{x1} = -z_1 \end{cases}\]
VIRTUAL 2
\[\begin{cases} T''_{y1} = 0 \\ M''_{x1} = -1 \end{cases}\]

Step 3: Internal Actions - Section 2 (\(0 \leq z_2 \leq \ell\))

REAL:

\[\begin{cases} T_{y2} = -F - R_{yA} \\ M_{x2} = -F \cdot z_2 - R_{yA}(\ell + z_2) - RM_{xA} \end{cases}\]
VIRTUAL 1
\[\begin{cases} T'_{y2} = -1 \\ M'_{x2} = -(\ell + z_2) \end{cases}\]
VIRTUAL 2
\[\begin{cases} T''_{y2} = 0 \\ M''_{x2} = -1 \end{cases}\]

Step 4: Apply PCVW - Virtual System 1

External virtual work:

\[\delta W_e = 0\]

Internal virtual work:

\[\delta W_i = \int_0^\ell M'_{x1} \cdot \frac{M_{x1}}{EJ_{xx}} \, dz_1 + \int_0^\ell M'_{x2} \cdot \frac{M_{x2}}{EJ_{xx}} \, dz_2 = 0\]

This yields the first equation relating \(R_{yA}\) and \(RM_{xA}\).

Step 5: Apply PCVW - Virtual System 2

External virtual work:

\[\delta W_e = 0\]

Internal virtual work:

\[\delta W_i = \int_0^\ell M''_{x1} \cdot \frac{M_{x1}}{EJ_{xx}} \, dz_1 + \int_0^\ell M''_{x2} \cdot \frac{M_{x2}}{EJ_{xx}} \, dz_2 = 0\]

This yields the second equation. Solving the system of two equations:

Final Answers: \[\boxed{R_{yA} = -2000 \text{ N}}\] \[\boxed{RM_{xA} = 1{,}000{,}000 \text{ N} \cdot \text{mm}}\]

3 Fixed-Slider, Point Load \(R_{yA} = -2000 \text{ N}\), \(RM_{xA} = 1{,}000{,}000 \text{ N·mm}\)
