Solution:

Step 1: Calculate section properties

\[J_{xx} = 2Ar^2 = 2 \times 400 \times 100^2 = 8 \times 10^6\, \text{mm}^4\]

\[S_{x_3} = -S_{x_1} = Ar = 400 \times 100 = 4 \times 10^4\, \text{mm}^3\]

Step 2: Calculate shear flows (dummy load \(T_y' = 1\))

\[q_1' = 0\]

\[q_2' = -T_y \frac{S_{x_1}}{J_{xx}} = T_y \frac{Ar}{2Ar^2} = \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{T_y}{r}\]

\[q_3' = q_2' = \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{T_y}{r}\]

Step 3: Moment equivalence for closed cell flow \(q^*\)

\[H_z = 2q^* \cdot \Omega_{cell} + \left(2q_3' \cdot \frac{l}{4} \cdot \Omega_{cell}\right) \cdot z\]

\[H_z = 2q^* \cdot \pi r^2 + \frac{1}{2} T_y \pi r \cdot z\]

\[q^* = \frac{H_z - \frac{1}{2}T_y \pi r}{2\pi r^2}\]

Step 4: Real and Dummy loading

Real: \(T_y = -F\), \(H_z = F \cdot r\), \(M_x = -F \cdot z\)

Dummy: \(T_y' = -1\), \(H_z' = -1 \cdot r\), \(M_x' = -z\)

Step 5: Virtual Work Method

\[\delta W_e = 1 \cdot v\]

\[\delta W_i = \int \delta\tau \cdot \frac{\tau}{G}\, dV + \int_0^l M_x' \cdot \frac{M_x}{EJ}\, dz\]

\[= \sum_i \int_0^l \frac{1}{2}\pi r \cdot t \cdot \frac{\delta q_i \cdot q_i}{Gt^2}\, dz + \int_0^l M_x' \cdot \frac{M_x}{EJ}\, dz\]

Final Answer: \(\boxed{v = 5.1919\, \text{mm}}\)

Solution:

Note: Consider only equivalence of internal actions when panel is open.

Step 1: Open section shear flows for unit \(F_y = 1\)

\[q_1' = \frac{F}{a}\]

\[q_2' = 0\]

Step 2: Apply \(F_y\) at shear center position \(x_{sc}\)

For moment equilibrium with closed cell flow \(q^*\):

\[F_y \cdot x_{sc} = q^* \cdot ab \cdot \frac{\text{perimeter}}{\ell}\]

\[q^* = \frac{F_y \cdot x_{sc}}{ab}\]

Step 3: Rotation condition for shear center (\(\theta' = 0\))

\[\theta' = \frac{1}{2Gt \cdot \Omega_{cell}} \cdot \left(-q_1' \cdot a + q_2' \cdot b + q^* \cdot \left(a + b + \sqrt{a^2 + b^2}\right)\right) = 0\]

Step 4: Solve with \(G = \frac{E}{2(1+\nu)}\)

\[G = \frac{72000}{2(1+0.3)} = 27692.3\, \text{MPa}\]

Final Answer: \(\boxed{x_{sc} = 34.86\, \text{mm}}\)

Solution:

Step 1: Define the hyperstatic unknown \(H_x\) (spring reaction)

Step 2: Equilibrium equations

\[X: H_x - F + R_{x_B} = 0\]

\[Y: R_{y_A} + R_{y_B} = 0\]

\[M@B: F \cdot a - H_x \cdot a - R_{y_A} \cdot b = 0\]

\[R_{y_A} = (F - H_x) \frac{a}{b}\]

Step 3: Internal forces for each segment

Segment \(z_1\): \(T_{z_1} = -(F - H_x)\frac{a}{b}\), \(T_{z_1}' = +\frac{a}{b}\)

Segment \(z_2\): \(T_{z_2} = -H_x\), \(T_{z_2}' = -1\)

\(M_{x_2} = -(F - H_x)\frac{a}{b} \cdot z_2\), \(M_{x_2}' = +\frac{a}{b} \cdot z_2\)

Segment \(z_3\): \(T_{z_3} = (F - H_x)\frac{a}{b}\), \(T_{z_3}' = -\frac{a}{b}\)

\(M_{x_3} = -H_x \cdot z_3 + F \cdot z_3 - (F - H_x)\frac{a}{b} \cdot b\), \(M_{x_3}' = -z_3 + \frac{a}{b} \cdot b\)

Step 4: Apply PVW (compatibility)

\[1 \cdot \left(-\frac{H_x}{K}\right) = \int_0^a \frac{(F-H_x)}{EA} \cdot \frac{a^2}{b^2}\, dz_1 + \int_0^b \frac{H_x}{EA} \cdot (-1) + \frac{(F-H_x)}{EJ} \cdot \frac{a^2}{b^2} \cdot z_2^2\, dz_2 + \ldots\]

Step 5: Solve for \(H_x\)

\[-\frac{H_x}{K} = \frac{H_x a^3}{EAb^2} + \frac{Fa^3}{EAb^2} + \frac{H_x}{EA} \cdot b + \frac{1}{3}\frac{H_x a^2 b^3}{EJb^4} - \frac{Fa^2 b^3}{3EJb^4} + \frac{H_x a^3}{EAb^2} - \frac{Fa^3}{EAb^2} + \ldots\]

Final Answer: \(\boxed{u = -\frac{H_x}{K} = -7.699 \times 10^{-2}\, \text{mm}}\)
